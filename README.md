# Kingdom of Chaos

An AI-assisted observatory documenting the strange, beautiful, funny and interconnected nature of everyday life.

This is not a journal.

This project observes life rather than driving life.

## Core Philosophy

- Curiosity over certainty
- Observation over assumption
- Patterns over narratives
- Humour over perfection
- Authenticity over optimisation

Question everything.
Follow nothing.
Seek truth.
Also answer your emails.
